Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship?

No I do not. It is illegal for me to ship my cookies under the Louisiana Cottage Food Law.

How much are your cookies?

Please see the Pricing tab at the top of the page to see the pricing guide on cookies.

How does payment work?

Half of your total is due at the time of booking as a deposit to hold the spot on my calender. The reminder of your total is due when you pickup. I accept Venmo, PayPal & cash.

Do you offer refunds?

I can refund your deposit up to 14 business days before the date of the pickup if you need to cancel your order.

How far in advance do I need to place my order?

Generally my calender books quickly. I try to update my availability every couple of months. Holiday seasons book faster because my availability is planned around holidays/specials. The sooner you book your order, the high possibility there is that I will be available to take your order.

How long do your cookies stay fresh?

All of my cookies are individually wrapped and heat sealed to ensure maximum freshness. They can be kept fresh for about in the heat sealed.

Can I freeze the cookies?

Absolutely! I have successfully frozen my cookies and had several customers who have done the same. Place the cookies (in their heat sealed bags) in a ziploc or airtight tub and place in freezer (if they were packaged in a box, you can just put the box in the freezer). Take the container out of the freezer the night before you need them and do not open the container until they have completely thawed to avoid ruining the cookies.
